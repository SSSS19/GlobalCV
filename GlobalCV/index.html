About GlobalCV

GlobalCV was born from a simple observation: the global job market isn't truly global when it comes to CVs.

International students graduating from universities worldwide face a unique challenge. Their education is internationally recognized, their skills are globally relevant, but their CVs? Often rejected before a human ever reads them—simply because they don't match local formatting expectations.

A CV that works perfectly in the United States might be completely wrong for Germany. What's essential in a Japanese CV could be illegal to include in a Canadian one. Length expectations, photo requirements, personal information norms—these vary dramatically from country to country.

We built GlobalCV to solve this problem. Our platform combines deep cultural knowledge of hiring practices across 35+ countries with AI-powered tools to help you create CVs that feel locally native, wherever you're applying.

Whether you're an international student planning your first job search, a professional considering opportunities abroad, or a migrant building a new career in a new country, GlobalCV ensures your qualifications are presented in the format that local employers expect and trust.

Our Mission

To remove formatting barriers from the global job market, ensuring that talent can compete on merit, not on their knowledge of local CV conventions.
